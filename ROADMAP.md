# TutoriAI Mathematics Roadmap

This roadmap focuses on building the TutoriAI platform initially for mathematics, specifically tailored to the International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme (IBDP) Mathematics: Analysis and Approaches at Standard Level (AASL) and Higher Level (AAHL). Future phases will extend support to other subjects.

## Phase 1: Planning and Setup
- Gather requirements from math tutors familiar with IBDP AASL/AAHL.
- Prioritize features needed for managing math students and generating math-specific resources.
- Design data models for students, tutors, schedules, and math resources.

## Phase 2: Core Features for Mathematics
- Implement authentication for tutors and students.
- Build dashboard views showing upcoming lessons and assignments.
- Integrate a calendar system for booking tutoring sessions.
- Provide CRUD operations for managing student profiles and progress in math topics.
- Create an assignment builder with templates aligned to the AASL and AAHL curriculum.

## Phase 3: Whiteboard and Collaboration
- Add a real-time whiteboard suitable for writing equations and drawing graphs.
- Use WebSockets or similar technology for live collaboration during tutoring sessions.
- Integrate video meeting links for virtual lessons.

## Phase 4: Math Resource Generation
- Include tools to generate worksheets and homework focused on AASL/AAHL topics such as calculus, algebra, statistics, and probability.
- Allow tutors to customize questions or pull from a question bank organized by IB syllabus sections.
- Offer downloadable or shareable PDF exports of assignments.

## Phase 5: Analytics and Progress Tracking
- Track completion of assignments and sessions for each student.
- Provide reports showing progress across AASL or AAHL syllabus content.
- Enable feedback loops so tutors can adjust lesson plans based on student performance.

## Phase 6: Future Subject Expansion
- After refining the math-focused features, generalize the platform so additional subjects can reuse scheduling, resource generation, and analytics components.
- Maintain subject-specific templates and question banks while sharing common infrastructure.
